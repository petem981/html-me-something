A Little Something About Mary

Allow me introduce myself: My name is Mary and I come from a very small town in a very southern part of the state of Louisiana. I guess one could say I'm Cajun, but my accent isn't what anyone would expect. How's that? Well, I'm not sure really. But I think it has something to do with my travels, education and life experiences.
Military for 10 years that provided me with all sorts of experience. And as I retired, I decided to re-enter the world of all things equine. Because that's where my heart truly lives.
To coding, my newest endeavor. What do I want out of it? Be able to work flexibly to raise my family and to afford my hobby of horsemanship.
